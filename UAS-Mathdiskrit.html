Hitunglah Clossenes Centrality(G)!#

Rumus: \(C_C(v_i) = \left[ \frac{1}{n-1} \sum_{j \neq i} g(v_i, v_j) \right]^{-1} = \frac{n-1}{\sum_{j \neq i} g(v_i, v_j)}\)

Answer#

Diket: \(v_i\)=G n = 7 (Banyaknya Node) Jarak Terpendek Dari Node G G to A = 2 G to B = 1 G to C = 3 G to D = 2 G to E = 3 G to F = 1 \({\sum_{j \neq i} g(v_i, v_j)}\) = 12

  • \(C_C(G)\) = \(\frac{7-1}{12}\)

  • \(C_C(G)\) = \(\frac{6}{12}\) = 0,5 atau \(\frac{1}{2}\)

Hitunglah Betweennes Centrality(F)!#

Rumus: \(C_B(v) = \sum_{s \neq v \neq t} \frac{\sigma_{st}(v)}{\sigma_{st}}\)

Answer#

Diket: \(v\) = F Jarak antara node yang melewati F : A to G = 1/1 B to G = 2/2 C to G = 2/2 D to G = 3/3 E to G = 2/2 Jumlah Jalur Terpendek = 5 \(C_B(F)\) = 5
